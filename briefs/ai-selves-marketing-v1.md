## ANIMATION STYLE NOTES

**Overall Vibe:**
- Fast, punchy cuts
- Slack/Discord aesthetic but elevated
- Dark mode (#111111 base)
- Pika brand colors for accents (purple #C2BEFF, yellow #FEFBCF)
- Text animations: slide-in, type-on, scale pops
- Reactions: bouncy spring physics
- Transitions: hard cuts mostly, one time-lapse for overnight sequence

**Typography:**
- UI: Space Mono (monospace, techy)
- End card: Telka Extended (bold, impactful)

**Audio (suggestions):**
- Notification sounds (subtle, not annoying)
- Keyboard clicks for typing
- Reaction pop sounds
- Music: upbeat, techy, builds to end
- Hard stop on cut to end card
