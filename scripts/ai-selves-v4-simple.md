## EDIT NOTES

**What changed from v3:**
- Removed ALL motion graphics, 3D camera, parallax
- First-person narration (not third-person VO)
- The "twist" structure â€” audience thinks these are humans until the reveal
- Every visual is a real Slack screenshot, simple hard cut
- Text cards are plain white on black â€” no animation beyond fade
- The emotional weight comes from the STORY not the edit

**The twist is everything.** The audience spends 38 seconds thinking "wow, what a great team" â€” then realizes these are AI. That reframe is the whole ad. It proves the point better than any feature list ever could: these AI selves feel REAL.

**Music:** One piano. One instrument. Nothing else until the very end where a soft pad can swell underneath. The simplicity mirrors the edit.

**Pacing rule:** When in doubt, hold longer. Silence > filler. Let the screenshots do the talking. Let the VO breathe. The gaps between words carry the emotion.

**The selfie moment (Beat 4)** should make the audience feel something before the reveal. If they don't feel anything during Nyx's check-in, the twist won't land. Protect this moment. Give it time.
